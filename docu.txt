chem.py is a suite of tools designed to make chem hw less tedious.

to use, enter

python chem.py [command]
in the terminal.

(Note that chem.py requires Python3. If the command to invoke the updated version is different, 
e.g. python3, then use it instead of `python`, e.g. python3 chem.py [command]).

The possible formats of command are as follows:

mole

A collection of commands related to molar masses.

python3 chem.py mole [-g | -m] COMPOUND
	Handles molar masses.

python3 chem.py ptable [-a | -s | -n]
